Cherry Blossom Flowers

A cherry blossom is a flower of many trees of genus Prunus or Prunus subg. Cerasus. They are known as yinghua (櫻花) in Chinese and Japanese cherry and sakura (桜 or 櫻; さくら or サクラ) in Japanese.They are also a common species in East Asia, including China, Korea, and especially in Japan.

Cherry Daisy Flowers

The daisy is one of the two official birth flowers of the month of April (the other being the sweet pea) but is perfect to give someone in a bouquet at any time of the year.With its fragile white petals and vibrant golden center, the daisy is known for its beauty as well as its symbolic meaning.

Sunflowers

Helianthus is a genus comprising about 70 species of annual and perennial flowering plants in the daisy family Asteraceae commonly known as sunflowers.Sunflowers attract bees, making them useful in areas where people are beekeeping and looking to attract more bees.

Photo by Chad Spacey www.pexels.com

Pink Hibiscus Flowers

Hibiscus is a genus of flowering plants in the mallow family, Malvaceae. The genus is quite large, comprising several hundred species that are native to warm temperate, subtropical and tropical regions throughout the world.Pink hibiscus blossoms often symbolize friendship and various types of love, such as platonic or familial. Giving someone a pink hibiscus flower is a way to express that you care about them.
